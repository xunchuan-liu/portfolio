I'm Xunchuan, a fourth-year undergraduate student studying computer science at Northwestern.

My work has primarily focused on web design, web development, and motion graphics. I am interested in using design and technology as a way to create seamless user experiences and tell meaningful stories.

I am currently working as a web designer with the Indigenous STEAM research group and as a web developer with the Querrey Simpson Institute for Bioelectronics.

Outside of work, you might find me playing music (or just humming), cooking a recipe (for disaster), or traveling somewhere (or nowhere).
